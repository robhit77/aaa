Real California nonprofits

Where your play goes

Every correct answer helps fund a donation to a real California nonprofit — and you pick which cause. Most of them are right here on the Central Coast; two are in Santa Maria itself.

Rice — fight hunger

Supports the Foodbank of Santa Barbara County, which works to end hunger across the county from its Santa Maria and Goleta sharehouses.

Beans — stock the food bank

Also supports the Foodbank of Santa Barbara County and the 300-plus local pantries and programs it supplies.

Kibble — feed shelter animals

Supports Santa Barbara Humane, caring for dogs and cats at its Santa Maria and Santa Barbara campuses since 1887.

Ocean — clean up plastic

Supports the San Luis Obispo Chapter of the Surfrider Foundation and its Central Coast beach cleanups.

Trees — grow a forest

Supports TreePeople, which plants and cares for trees across Southern California.

Free Aid for Answers donates to these organizations. They are not affiliated with, and do not sponsor or endorse, Free Aid for Answers.

Why it's free, and who runs it

The donations don't come out of your pocket — they're funded by the ads and sponsors on the site. That's the whole idea behind play-to-donate games like this one: you generate the value by showing up and playing, and that value pays for the giving. Free Aid for Answers is independently run and transparent by design — we tell you exactly how it's funded and name every nonprofit your play supports.

Free Financial Literacy Quiz

What is a financial literacy quiz?

Test how much you really know about money — for free. This financial literacy quiz covers the everyday things school tends to skip: budgeting, saving, credit, taxes, and more. Every question you answer right also helps fund a real charity, so you're building your own money smarts and giving back at the same time. No account, no cost, no catch.

What will I learn from this quiz?

The questions move through the money skills that actually come up in real life:

  • Budgeting & spending — where your money goes and how to steer it.
  • Saving & emergency funds — how much cushion you really need.
  • Credit & debt — interest, scores, and staying out of trouble.
  • Taxes, made simple — the basics, minus the jargon.
  • Investing fundamentals — how money grows over time.
Who is this quiz for?
  • Students building money skills before they need them.
  • Teachers who want a free, no-login classroom tool. (See the community service hours page for the teacher toolkit and certificates.)
  • Anyone who wants a quick, honest gut-check on what they know.
How does the quiz give back?

This isn't a quiz that just hands you a score. Every correct answer helps fund a cause you choose — fighting hunger, feeding shelter animals, cleaning the ocean, or planting trees. The ads and sponsors on the site cover the donations, so it stays free for you.

Is it accurate? And is it really free?

Our financial questions are reviewed by a licensed tax professional. These questions are written and checked for accuracy, so you're learning the real thing — not folk wisdom — and the whole quiz is free.
