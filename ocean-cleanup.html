Help Clean Plastic From the Ocean

Help clean plastic from the ocean — for free. Answer questions in the ocean game, and every correct answer helps fund Central Coast ocean cleanup and coastal protection. There's no donation required and no catch; the ads and sponsors on the site cover the cost. You just play.

Plastic doesn't break down — it breaks up, into smaller and smaller pieces that wash onto our beaches and into the water animals depend on. Removing it takes hands, organization, and funding. A California beach cleanup doesn't run itself, and this is a free way to help fund that work from wherever you are.

Your play supports the San Luis Obispo Chapter of the Surfrider Foundation, the local arm of a national coastal-protection nonprofit, running beach cleanups and ocean-protection efforts right here on the Central Coast. We donate to the chapter; it is not affiliated with, and does not sponsor or endorse, Free Aid for Answers.

Answer financial literacy questions or general knowledge — every correct answer helps remove plastic from the ocean, and it's completely free.
