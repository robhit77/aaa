Sponsored learning with a local heart

Free Aid For Answers turns money questions into a small act of giving.

This is a lightweight financial literacy quiz where your answers matter twice — once for your own knowledge, and once for a real donation to a local Central Coast cause.

How It Works

Each session serves questions in random order across three difficulty levels. When you answer correctly, a token drops into the bowl and your count rises. When you miss one, the quiz shows the correct answer and keeps moving — no shame, just learning.

What It Covers

The question bank covers filing status, standard deductions, W-2s and 1099s, estimated taxes, self-employment, retirement accounts, credit scores, emergency funds, compound interest, budgeting basics, and common tax myths.

The Charity Commitment

Every session you play generates a donation to your chosen Central Coast organization. You pick from five local causes — food banks, waterway protection, animal welfare, or forest conservation — and your correct answers move the needle for real. Donations are made quarterly.

Local Impact First

Every cause on this quiz serves the Central Coast. Whether it's the Foodbank of Santa Barbara County, Santa Barbara Channelkeeper, or Los Padres ForestWatch — the organizations here are rooted in the same communities that use this quiz.

A Friendly Note

Free Aid For Answers is educational and general in nature. Tax rules can change, and personal situations vary. For decisions about your own return or finances, always consult a qualified tax or financial professional.
