Help Plant Trees — for Free

Help plant trees in California without spending a dime. Answer questions in the tree game, and every correct answer helps fund reforestation and tree planting across the state. It's a free way to plant trees and help grow a forest — just play.

Trees cool neighborhoods, clean the air, hold soil in place, and bring fire-scarred land back to life. California needs more of them, especially after each wildfire season. Planting them and caring for them takes years of work and steady funding, and this is a way to donate to plant trees for free — the ads and sponsors cover the cost, not you.

Your play supports TreePeople, one of Southern California's largest environmental nonprofits, which has planted and cared for millions of trees since 1973 — reforesting burned wildlands, greening neighborhoods, and distributing trees across the region. We donate to TreePeople; it is not affiliated with, and does not sponsor or endorse, Free Aid for Answers.

Answer financial literacy questions or general knowledge — every correct answer supports reforestation in California, at no cost to you.
