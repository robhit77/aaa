Free Financial Literacy Quiz

Test how much you really know about money — for free. This financial literacy quiz covers the everyday things school tends to skip: budgeting, saving, credit, taxes, and more. Every question you answer right also helps fund a real charity, so you're building your own money smarts and giving back at the same time. No account, no cost, no catch.

Start the quiz ↓

What you'll learn

The questions move through the money skills that actually come up in real life:

  • Budgeting & spending — where your money goes and how to steer it.
  • Saving & emergency funds — how much cushion you really need.
  • Credit & debt — interest, scores, and staying out of trouble.
  • Taxes, made simple — the basics, minus the jargon.
  • Investing fundamentals — how money grows over time.

Who it's for

  • Students building money skills before they need them.
  • Teachers who want a free, no-login classroom tool. (See the community service hours page for the teacher toolkit and certificates.)
  • Anyone who wants a quick, honest gut-check on what they know.

How it gives back

This isn't a quiz that just hands you a score. Every correct answer helps fund a cause you choose — fighting hunger, feeding shelter animals, cleaning the ocean, or planting trees. The ads and sponsors on the site cover the donations, so it stays free for you.

Accurate, and free

Our financial questions are reviewed by a licensed tax professional. These questions are written and checked for accuracy, so you're learning the real thing — not folk wisdom — and the whole quiz is free.
