Rice Donation Game

Welcome to the rice donation game — a free way to fight hunger without spending a cent. Answer questions, and every correct answer helps fund a donation toward feeding families right here on California's Central Coast. You bring what you know; the ads and sponsors on the site cover the cost. Think of it as donating rice to end hunger, except you do it by playing.

Hunger isn't a faraway problem. The Foodbank of Santa Barbara County reports that roughly one in four county residents rely on its network each year, and the Santa Maria Valley is one of the highest-need areas in the county. A rice game might sound small — but small adds up. A lot of people each answering a few questions becomes real help for neighbors who are stretched thin.

When you play to fight hunger here, your correct answers support the Foodbank of Santa Barbara County, which works to end hunger and improve nutrition countywide from its sharehouses in Santa Maria and Goleta, supplying more than 300 local pantries and meal programs. We donate to the Foodbank; it is not affiliated with, and does not sponsor or endorse, Free Aid for Answers.

Play with our financial literacy questions or general knowledge — either way, every right answer helps feed families in California, and it costs you nothing.
